## Capability stories (what we can demonstrably do)

### Story 1: Learning loops with evidence ratchets

**What we built:** futon1a's write pipeline has a counter-ratchet
on protected-class updates — state can only move forward, never
backwards. Every write goes through a layered pipeline (L4 model
validation → L3 authorization → L2 entity integrity → L1 identity
→ L0 durable write) with proof paths. The frame schema tracks
derivatives over time.

**Who needs this:** Anyone whose AI system needs to demonstrate
that it's getting better, not worse, over time. Regulated
industries (finance, healthcare, government) where "we can prove
the model hasn't regressed" is a compliance requirement.

**Warm contact connection:** "AI makes behaviour unpredictable" —
the ratchet is precisely a mechanism for making forward progress
provable even when individual steps are unpredictable.

### Story 2: Pattern-driven structured assessment

**What we built:** 856 patterns in flexiarg format (structured
IF/HOWEVER/THEN/BECAUSE argumentation). Semantic search via MiniLM
embeddings. Pattern Selection Records (PSR) document why a pattern
was chosen; Pattern Use Records (PUR) document what happened.

**Who needs this:** Anyone who needs to assess whether an AI system
meets criteria that can't be reduced to "did the output match the
expected string." Qualitative evaluation, methodology assessment,
design review.

**Warm contact connection:** "Testing needs new approaches" —
pattern-driven assessment IS a new approach. Instead of test cases,
you have structured arguments with evidence.

### Story 3: Multi-agent session evidence

**What we built:** futon3c logs every turn with author, session,
timestamp. Evidence certificates for context retrieval. Session
replay in the War Machine hex visualiser. WebArxana shows author
attribution.

**Who needs this:** Anyone running AI agents who needs to audit
what happened and why. AI governance, responsible AI, incident
response.

**Warm contact connection:** Directly addresses "reproducibility
in applied AI" — if you can replay the session with full evidence,
the behaviour becomes reproducible (or at least auditable).

### Story 4: Strategic synthesis from evidence

**What we built:** War Machine reads 13 observation channels from
live data, infers a strategic mode, detects constraint violations
(hermit trap, cargo warning), and produces actionable assessment.
The frame schema models instruments (options, futures, shorts,
yields) on a fictional quantity (mana).

**Who needs this:** Anyone managing a portfolio of AI-assisted work
who needs to know if things are improving. Portfolio managers,
research directors, CTOs.
